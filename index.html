Quick Decision Guide:

  • Must Have All Critical Items Checked: These are non-negotiable for safety and legal protection
  • Any Red Flags = Disqualify: These indicate serious problems that could endanger your loved one
  • Compare Multiple Providers: Interview at least 3 agencies before making a decision
  • Trust Your Instincts: If something feels off, investigate further or look elsewhere
  • Start with a Trial Period: Even the best interview doesn't guarantee good fit - start small
Next Steps After Using This Checklist: Compare your completed checklists side by side, call references for your top 2-3 choices, verify all licensing and insurance information independently, and consider starting with a short-term trial period before committing to long-term care arrangements.
